A **frontend developer** is a programmer who focuses on building the visual and interactive elements of a website or web application. They use technologies like **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** to create user-friendly interfaces that ensure a smooth and engaging experience. Frontend developers often work with frameworks such as **React, Angular, or Vue.js** to enhance functionality and improve performance. Their role involves collaborating with designers and backend developers to bring a project to life, making sure it is both aesthetically pleasing and fully functional across different devices and browsers.
